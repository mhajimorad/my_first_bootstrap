When it comes to teaching, I have often found that "less is more."

That is, following the KIS[S] motto goes a long way. In other words, Keep It Simple [...]!

  • Primarily cover content that is absolutely needed
  • Utilize images to provide context to content, ideally weaving/connecting different concepts together

A similar mindset should be employed if one desires to create effective websites

Color choices should be intentional

Better utilization of color

  • Sticking to a color
  • and its corresponding shades
  • utilizes color in a more intentional manner

Worse utilization of color

  • Alternating colors can be distracting
  • and shift attention away
  • from the content

Font choices should ensure readability

Use contrasting font weight to separate title from list items

  • Larger font size is more readable
  • and too small can detract visitors from reading the content
  • Text should be used minimally and optimally
  • and NOT going on and on and on and on and on and on ...
